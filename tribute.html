Elon Musk

  • June 28, 1971: Born in South Africa
  • Age 12: Creates and writes a video game called Blastar; sells it for the equivalent of $500
  • 1988: Graduates from Pretoria Boys High School with distinctions in science and computer studies
  • 1989 to 1991: Attends college at Queen’s University in Kingston, Ontario. Then transfers to the University of Pennsylvania; completed a BS in Economics (Wharton) and a BA with a major in physics
  • 1995: Moves to Silicon Valley; Leaves Stanford PhD program and Starts Zip2 with his brother, coding it himself and using a $28,000 investment from his Father
  • February 1999: Sells Zip2 to Compaq, the personal computer company, for $307 million, of which $22 million went to Musk.
  • March 1999: Starts online bank X.com
  • March 2000: X.com merges with Confinity to become PayPal
  • July 2002: eBay acquires PayPal for $1.5 billion in stock, of which $165 million goes to Musk
  • June 2002: Founds SpaceX to build his own rockets
  • 2004: Invests in Tesla Motors
  • Augusty 2008: Becomes first investor in SolarCity
  • October 2008: Becomes Tesla’s CEO
  • December 2008: SpaceX wins $1.6 billion NASA contract to bring cargo to the International Space Station
  • March 2009: Unveils Tesla Model S
  • Jun 29, 2010: Tesla goes public for $12 per share closes the day at $24
  • September 2011: Announces reusable rockets makes space flight 100 times cheaper
  • February 2012: Unveils Tesla Model X (SUV)
  • May 2012: SpaceX becomes the first commercial vehicle to deliver a load of supplies to the International Space Station.
  • June 2012: Tesla begins deliveries of the all-electric Model S
  • September 2012: Launches the Tesla Supercharger Network allowing for Long Distance Driving
  • Begins attempting to land a Dummy Rocket from low altitude
  • August 2013: Releases sketch and concept of the Hyperloop High Speed Rail
  • Attempts soft water landing of a rocket from Orbit
  • April 2014: Attempts to land a rocket on a ship from orbit
